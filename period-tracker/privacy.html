Privacy Policy

Luna — Cycle & Period Tracker

Effective date: April 2026

Luna collects nothing. Your cycle data never leaves your device — no servers, no cloud, no accounts, no exceptions.

What data Luna stores

Luna stores the following data locally on your iPhone only using Apple's standard on-device storage (AsyncStorage). This data is never transmitted anywhere:

  • ● Cycle dates — period start and end dates, flow intensity (light, medium, heavy)
  • ● Symptoms — physical symptoms you choose to log (e.g. cramps, headache, bloating)
  • ● Mood — your daily mood rating (1–5 scale)
  • ● Energy — your daily energy level (low, medium, high)
  • ● Notes — free-form text notes you write for any given day
  • ● App settings — your cycle length, period length, and goal preference
  • ● Onboarding status — whether you've completed the first-launch flow

What Luna does NOT collect

  • ✕ No personal information — no name, email, phone number, or any identifier
  • ✕ No analytics or usage data — we don't track screens visited, features used, or time spent
  • ✕ No crash reporting — no third-party crash reporting SDK is included in the app
  • ✕ No advertising identifiers — no IDFA or any advertising framework
  • ✕ No location data — Luna never requests location access
  • ✕ No health kit access — Luna does not read from or write to Apple Health
  • ✕ No camera or microphone access — not requested, not used

How your data is stored

All data is stored on your device using React Native AsyncStorage, which writes to your iPhone's standard app sandboxed storage. This data:

  • ● Stays on your device and is not synced to iCloud
  • ● Is not accessible to other apps on your device
  • ● Is not transmitted over the internet at any time
  • ● Is protected by your iPhone's built-in security and encryption
About fonts: Luna uses fonts bundled directly in the app binary. No external font services (like Google Fonts) are loaded at runtime. The app functions completely offline.

No third-party SDKs

Luna does not include any third-party SDKs that could collect or transmit data, including but not limited to:

  • ✕No Firebase or Google Analytics
  • ✕No Facebook SDK or Meta Pixel
  • ✕No Sentry, Bugsnag, or any crash reporter
  • ✕No AppsFlyer, Adjust, or attribution SDK
  • ✕No Mixpanel, Amplitude, or product analytics

Luna uses only standard Expo and React Native libraries for rendering the UI. These libraries run entirely on-device and do not make network requests.

Deleting your data

You have full control over your data at all times:

  • ● In-app deletion: Go to Settings → "Delete All Data" to permanently erase all stored entries, settings, and onboarding state
  • ● Uninstall: Deleting the Luna app from your iPhone removes all associated data

Because no data is stored on any server, there is no account to request deletion of and no need to contact us to remove your data.

Children's privacy

Luna is intended for users aged 12 and older. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone — the app stores data only on the user's device and transmits nothing. There are no accounts, no registration, and no user-identifiable information at any point.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how Luna handles data — which would require a significant departure from our privacy-first design philosophy — we will update this policy and post a clear notice within the app. The effective date at the top of this page reflects when it was last updated.

Given that Luna stores nothing externally, any change significant enough to affect your privacy would also require your active consent within the app before taking effect.
