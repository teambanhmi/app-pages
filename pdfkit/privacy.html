Privacy Policy

Folio — Sign, Fill & Annotate PDFs — Team Banh Mi — Effective April 16, 2026

Short version: Folio collects no personal data, requires no account, and never transmits your documents anywhere. Everything stays on your device.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Folio does and does not collect when you use the app.

1. What Data We Collect

None. Folio does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or sign-in is required or offered.
  • No analytics or crash-reporting SDKs are included in the app.
  • No usage statistics, telemetry, or behavioral data are sent anywhere.
  • No advertising identifiers (IDFA) are accessed or used.

2. Your Documents

All PDF files you import, create, annotate, merge, or scan are stored exclusively on your device using Apple's standard app sandbox (the app's Documents directory). Your files are:

  • Never uploaded to any server.
  • Never shared with third parties.
  • Never backed up to any cloud service controlled by us (standard iCloud device backup is controlled entirely by you through iOS Settings).

3. In-App Purchase

Folio offers a one-time in-app purchase ("Folio Pro") processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit 2. We do not receive your payment information. Apple's Privacy Policy governs that transaction.

The app stores a local record of your purchase status using Apple's StoreKit transaction APIs. This record stays on your device.

4. Camera Access

Folio requests camera permission only when you use the Document Scanner feature. Camera access is used solely to capture images for conversion to PDF. No images are stored beyond your immediate scanning session unless you save the resulting PDF to your document library.

You can revoke camera permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera.

5. iCloud & Device Backups

Folio does not use iCloud Drive or any cloud sync service. If you have enabled standard iCloud device backups in iOS Settings, Apple may back up the app's local data (your PDFs and settings) as part of your device backup — this is controlled entirely by you, not by us.

6. Third-Party SDKs & Services

Folio uses only Apple's built-in frameworks: PDFKit, PencilKit, VisionKit, and StoreKit. No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are embedded.

7. Children's Privacy

Folio does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Because we collect no data at all, COPPA requirements do not apply to our practices.

8. Data Deletion

To delete all data associated with Folio, simply delete the app from your device. This permanently removes all PDFs, annotations, signatures, backups, and settings stored by the app. There is no server-side data to request deletion of.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our data practices in a meaningful way, we will update this page and the effective date above. Any change that involves collecting personal data would be announced prominently before taking effect.

10. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at admin@quietmuse.org.
