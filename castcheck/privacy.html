Privacy Policy

CastCheck — Team Banh Mi — Effective March 19, 2026

CastCheck is a movie and TV show reference app. This policy explains what data the app does and does not collect.

Data We Do Not Collect

CastCheck does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or login is required.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any identifying information.
  • We do not use analytics, crash reporting services, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not track your usage or behavior.
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties.

Third-Party Data: TMDB

CastCheck fetches movie and TV show information from The Movie Database (TMDB) via their public API. When you search for or browse content, your device makes network requests to TMDB's servers. These requests are subject to TMDB's Privacy Policy. CastCheck does not send any personal data to TMDB beyond what is inherent in a standard HTTPS request (e.g., your IP address).

Local Storage

CastCheck may store data locally on your device (such as favorites or recently viewed items) solely for your convenience. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Children's Privacy

CastCheck is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
