Privacy Policy

VialCalc — Team Banh Mi — Effective April 15, 2026

VialCalc is a peptide reconstitution calculator for iOS. This policy explains what data the app collects, where it is stored, and what it does not collect.

Short version: VialCalc stores your calculation history on your device only. It never connects to the internet, never collects personal information, and contains no analytics or tracking of any kind.

Data the App Collects

VialCalc stores one type of data locally on your device:

  • Calculation history — the inputs you enter (vial amount, BAC water volume, desired dose, peptide name) and the results calculated from them. This data is stored using Apple's SwiftData framework in your app's private on-device container.

This data is created by you, for you. It never leaves your device.

Where Data Is Stored

All data is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Specifically:

  • No data is sent to any server.
  • No data is stored in iCloud or any cloud service.
  • No account or login is required.
  • The data is stored in your app's private container, accessible only to VialCalc.

What the App Does NOT Collect

VialCalc makes no network requests. The following are explicitly absent from the app:

  • No analytics SDKs (no Firebase, no Amplitude, no Mixpanel, or any equivalent)
  • No crash reporting services (no Crashlytics, no Sentry, or any equivalent)
  • No advertising SDKs or ad networks
  • No usage tracking or behavioral telemetry of any kind
  • No collection of your name, email address, or any identifying information
  • No collection of your IP address or location
  • No third-party code of any kind — the app has zero external dependencies

Deleting Your Data

You have full control over your calculation history:

  • In-app deletion — open the History tab and delete individual records or all history at any time.
  • Uninstall — uninstalling VialCalc from your device permanently deletes all locally stored data. Because data is never sent anywhere, uninstalling is a complete and permanent deletion.

Children's Privacy

VialCalc is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
