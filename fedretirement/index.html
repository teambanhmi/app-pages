Frequently asked questions

What is my MRA?

Your Minimum Retirement Age is the earliest age you can retire under FERS with at least 30 years of service. It depends on your birth year: 55 for pre-1948 births, stepping up to 57 for 1970+ births. Fed Retirement looks it up automatically from your date of birth using the OPM table.

Does this app work for FERS-Special (LEO, Firefighter, ATC)?

Yes. FERS-Special employees in covered positions can retire at 50 with 20 years (or any age with 25 years). The app models the 1.7% tier for the first 20 covered years and 1.0% thereafter, plus COLA from retirement (not age 62) per 5 USC §8462(c)(2)(B), plus the age-62 recomputation that imputes disability-period service.

What about WEP and GPO?

Both the Windfall Elimination Provision and the Government Pension Offset were repealed by the Social Security Fairness Act (P.L. 118-273), signed January 2025 and retroactive to January 2024. If you have Social Security credits from non-federal employment (common for CSRS retirees with side work), you may now be eligible for unreduced benefits. Enter your SSA estimate — we don't apply a reduction.

Can I import my FERS Statement data?

Not in v1.0. All inputs are manual — Service Computation Date from your SF-50, SS estimates from ssa.gov, TSP balance from tsp.gov. This is deliberate: we don't want to hold a credential or scrape another site. v1.1 may add FERS Statement OCR if enough users request it.

Is my data private?

Yes. The app runs 100% on-device. There is no account. There is no cloud sync in v1.0. There is no analytics SDK. There is no network call at any point during calculation. Delete the app and your data is gone.
